PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

BITSTART.CLUB is designed as a "No-Data" platform. We do not require registration, accounts, or any personal information to access our games. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not use trackers to build personal profiles. We do not store your browsing history or game performance on our central servers. All game high scores and progress are stored locally in your browser's Cache/Local Storage and never leave your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Google)

To keep our games free, we partner with third-party providers:

  • Google AdSense: This service uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics: We use this to understand general traffic patterns (e.g., which games are most popular). This data is aggregated and anonymized.

4. Cookies

We do not set our own tracking cookies. However, third-party services mentioned above may place cookies on your browser. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is family-friendly. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are fully compliant with COPPA and GDPR regarding the privacy of minors.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out via our support page.
