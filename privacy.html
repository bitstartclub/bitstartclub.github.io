PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

1. Introduction

BITSTART.CLUB is a privacy-focused gaming platform. While we do not require user accounts or collect personal identification like names or emails, we use industry-standard third-party tools to maintain our services. This policy explains how data is handled by these partners.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

To keep our games free, we use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. Analytics and Performance

We use Google Analytics to monitor aggregate traffic patterns. This helps us understand which games are popular and improve performance. This data is processed in a way that does not identify individuals but may include technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers.

4. Data Storage and Local Cache

Your game progress and high scores are stored exclusively using Local Storage and IndexDB within your web browser. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by BITSTART.CLUB servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache.

5. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Under the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), you have the right to:

  • Object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes.
  • Request that third-party partners (like Google) delete or restrict access to your technical identifiers.
  • Opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information as defined by CCPA.

6. Children's Privacy

Our content is designed for a general audience. Because we do not collect personal information (PII), we operate in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

7. Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in ad network requirements. Continued use of the site implies acceptance of these terms.
